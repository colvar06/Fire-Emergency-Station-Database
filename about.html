About this website

Fire Rescue Station Database is a research tool for looking up fire department, district or agency information. This includes address, parent company, hours, phone-number for non-emergency calls and website of fire agency. Our current projection on completion of the first phase of launch is include Tualatin Fire stations 19 of Midway, station 20 of downtown Newberg, station 21 of outskirts of Newberg and station 33 of central Sherwood, Yamhill County Communications Station 1 of downtown Mcminnville, station 4 of Yamhill, station 5 of Amity, station 6 of Dayton, station 7 of Carlton and station 10 of Lafayette, St. Paul Downtown station and finally Gaston Rural district station.

We possibly will include more stations extending to multiple agencys once we get the first phase finished.
